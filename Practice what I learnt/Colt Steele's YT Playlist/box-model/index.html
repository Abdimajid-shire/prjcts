inline vs block vs inline- block

the width & height properties can't be changed in inline elements.
block elements take the entire line.
inline elements only take the space needed for their content. they also can be given only horizontal padding & margin
inline-block are inline elements with changeable widths & heights and can be given both horizontal & vertical margin and padding
